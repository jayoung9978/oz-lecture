The WWW project

The WorldWideWeb (W3) is a wide-area hypermedia information retrieval initiative aiming to give universal access to a large universal of documents.

Everything there is online about W3 is linked directly or indirectly to this doucument, including an executive summary of the project Mailing lists , policy , November's W3 news , Frequently Asked Question.
